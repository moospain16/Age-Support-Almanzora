Befriending Service

Loneliness can affect many people as they grow older. Our befriending service connects friendly volunteers with people in the community who would enjoy regular conversation and companionship.

Volunteers may visit in person or stay in touch through regular phone calls. These small acts of kindness can make a huge difference to someone’s wellbeing.

If you or someone you know would benefit from this service, please contact us.
