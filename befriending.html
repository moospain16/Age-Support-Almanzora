Befriending Service

Our befriending service helps reduce loneliness by connecting volunteers with people who would benefit from regular conversation and companionship.

What is Befriending?

Many elderly people in the community live alone or may not have regular opportunities to socialise. Our befriending volunteers provide friendly contact through visits or phone calls.

A simple chat, a friendly face or a regular phone call can make a big difference to someone's wellbeing and sense of connection.

How It Works

Our volunteers are carefully matched with people who would benefit from companionship. The support may include:

  • Regular friendly phone calls
  • Occasional home visits
  • Conversation and companionship
  • Helping people stay connected with the community

Get in Touch

If you or someone you know would benefit from our befriending service, please contact us. We will be happy to discuss how we can help.
