Friendly Support for Those Feeling Lonely

Our Befriending Service connects volunteers with people who would benefit from a friendly visit or phone call.

Loneliness can affect anyone, especially as we grow older or experience changes in health or mobility. A friendly conversation can make a huge difference.

Our volunteers provide companionship, conversation and support in a safe and caring way.
