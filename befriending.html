Befriending Service

Many older people experience loneliness, especially if they live alone or are far from family. Our Befriending Service connects you with friendly volunteers who care.

Would you like someone to talk to?

Call our team today

📞 634 316 573

How it Works

  • A friendly volunteer will contact you.
  • You can arrange regular phone calls or visits.
  • You decide what works best for you.

Who Can Use This Service?

  • Older people living alone
  • People who feel isolated
  • Carers who need support

Our Volunteers

All volunteers are caring members of the community who want to help older people feel connected and supported.
