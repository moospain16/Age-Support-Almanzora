Equipment Loan Service

We loan mobility equipment to people in the Almanzora Valley who need temporary or permanent support to maintain their independence and quality of life.

What We Offer

We have a range of mobility equipment available for loan, including:

  • Wheelchairs and ramps
  • Walking frames and rollators
  • Walking sticks and crutches
  • Bath and raised toilet seats
  • Commodes and bed tables

How It Works

Getting equipment is simple:

  • Contact us to discuss your needs
  • We will help you find suitable equipment
  • Equipment is delivered where possible
  • There is no cost for loans

Get in Touch

If you need equipment support, please contact us. Our team will be happy to discuss your requirements and find a solution.
