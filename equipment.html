Equipment Loan Service

We provide mobility and support equipment to help people remain safe, independent and comfortable in their own homes.

Equipment We Can Provide

Through donations and community support, Age Support Almanzora is able to loan a range of useful mobility and support equipment. These include..

  • Wheelchairs
  • Walking frames
  • Mobility aids
  • ..and other support equipment

How the Loan Service Works

Equipment is loaned to people who need temporary assistance due to illness, injury or reduced mobility.

If the equipment is no longer required, we kindly ask that it is returned so it can help another member of the community.

Request Equipment

If you or someone you know may benefit from borrowing equipment, please contact us and we will do our best to assist.
