Support Us

Age Support Almanzora is a registered charity run entirely by volunteers.

Every donation we receive goes directly towards supporting elderly people in the Almanzora Valley.

Why Your Support Matters

We are entirely self-funded and rely on the generosity of people like you. Your donation helps us provide:

  • Befriending visits and telephone support for isolated elderly people
  • Mobility equipment loans such as wheelchairs, walkers and walking frames
  • Carer support groups for those looking after a loved one
  • Information and advice on benefits and local services

Without donations, we simply could not continue. Every amount, however small, makes a real difference.

Donate Mobility Equipment

We also welcome donations of mobility aids and equipment. If you have items that are no longer in use and gathering dust, please consider donating them so that others in the Almanzora Valley can benefit.

We are particularly in need of:

  • Walking sticks and crutches
  • Walking frames and rollators
  • Wheelchairs and ramps
  • Bath seats and raised toilet seats
  • Commodes and bed tables
To arrange a donation of equipment, please call us on 634 316 573.

Thank You
