Volunteer With Us

Age Support Almanzora is run entirely by volunteers. Without the help of our volunteers we would not be able to support older people in the Almanzora Valley.

Volunteering can be a rewarding way to meet people, give back to the community, and make a real difference in someone’s life.

Interested in Volunteering?

Call us to learn more

📞 634 316 573

Ways You Can Help

  • Befriending older people who feel lonely
  • Helping at the information centre
  • Assisting with equipment loans
  • Helping at charity events or fundraisers

No Experience Needed

You do not need any special skills to volunteer. All you need is a little time and a caring attitude.

Even a small amount of time can make a huge difference to someone who feels isolated.
